Questions

FAQ

Is this a course?

No. It’s a beautifully organized digital guide (PDF). No videos to watch, no modules to fall behind on. Download it, open the protocol you need, use it tonight.

What ages is it for?

Built for toddlers and preschoolers, ages 2–5. Many of the scripts and printables work for younger toddlers and older kids too — the method is the same, only the words get simpler or bigger.

I’ve already tried scripts.

The script is only step one. This system shows you what to do when your child ignores it, escalates, or melts down harder.

What if it doesn’t work for my kid?

The goal is not a perfect toddler overnight. It’s a calm, consistent plan so you know exactly what to do next — including when a behavior isn’t misbehavior at all (hunger, pain, fear, overstimulation), and when to seek professional guidance.
